# EU4 国家 tag、中文名与史实纹章配色参考

These are ORIGINAL heraldic color choices (historical tinctures), used to draw
shield color-blocks. They are NOT extracted EU4 flag textures — those are Paradox
copyright and must never be ripped or redistributed. When a country isn't listed,
pick colors from its real historical coat of arms.

`c1` = primary (left half of shield), `c2` = secondary/overlay (right half).
Shield letter = first letter of the tag unless overridden (e.g. Ottoman → "O").

Tier conventions for the timeline (drives right-side color + label):
- `大` (red #d4423e)  — 大国对撞 : war vs a great power (FRA, OTT, big coalitions)
- `中` (orange #d98a2b) — 中等战争 : mid-size or two-front war
- `小` (green #7a8a5c)  — 边境冲突 : border skirmish vs minors
